.🧠 ALZ-AI: Multi-Modal Intelligence SuiteDiagnostics & Therapeutics for Alzheimer’s DiseaseALZ-AI is an end-to-end medical intelligence platform designed to bridge the gap between clinical diagnosis and pharmaceutical research. By integrating Dual-Attention Computer Vision, Interpretable ML, and Multi-Task Molecular Binding, it provides a 360° toolset for neurologists and researchers.🏛️ The Triple-Pillar Architecture1. Neuroimaging Engine (MRI Analysis) 🖼️Core Architecture: Implements Multi-Instance Learning (MIL) with a Dual-Attention ResNet-18.Format Flexibility: Native support for DICOM and NIfTI (.nii) 3D volumes.Smart Slicing: Automatically extracts the 16 most informative anatomical slices for high-speed inference.Explainable AI (XAI): Built-in Grad-CAM suite generates heatmaps, providing a "Proof of Decision" for radiologists.2. Clinical Risk Stratification 📊Logic: A "glass-box" Decision Tree Classifier analyzing 31 patient variables.Weighted Heuristics: Real-time risk levels (High/Moderate/Low) based on MMSE scores correlated with physiological factors.Production Ready: Serialized via joblib for sub-millisecond inference in hospital environments.3. Drug Repurposing & Molecular Binding 💊Stage 1 (Discovery): Cosine Similarity matching against Alzheimer's-related GPCR protein targets.Stage 2 (Validation): A Multi-Task Residual Network predicting four key metrics: $K_i$, $K_d$, $IC_{50}$, and $EC_{50}$.Stability: Uses Layer Normalization and Residual Blocks to navigate high-dimensional molecular latent spaces.🛠️ Tech StackLayerTechnologiesFrontendReact.js (Vite), Tailwind CSS, GSAP, Lucide IconsBackendFastAPI, Uvicorn, RESTful APIAI/MLPyTorch, TorchVision, Scikit-Learn, TransformersDevOpsDocker, Docker-Compose📂 Project Resources & Quick StartThe /examples directory is pre-loaded with data to get you started immediately:📂 MRI Test Suite: Sample .nii volumes and .zip archives of 2D slices.🧪 SMILES Library: Molecular strings ready to be pasted into the Drug Discovery dashboard.📋 Clinical Profiles: JSON payloads representing different patient demographics.⚙️ InstallationBash# 1.
